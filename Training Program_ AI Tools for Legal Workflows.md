# Training Program: AI Tools for Legal Workflows

## Program Overview

**Duration:** 14-16 weeks (3-4 weeks per phase)  
**Target Audience:** Legal professionals, paralegals, compliance officers, and law students  
**Prerequisites:** Basic legal knowledge, understanding of legal document structure, familiarity with legal research methodologies  
**Learning Format:** Hands-on workshops, ethics seminars, mock client scenarios, and confidentiality-focused training

## Program Goal

Train legal professionals to strategically integrate AI tools into legal workflows while maintaining the highest standards of professional ethics, client confidentiality, attorney-client privilege, and legal accuracy. Focus on enhancing efficiency and quality while preserving the fundamental principles of legal practice and professional responsibility.
