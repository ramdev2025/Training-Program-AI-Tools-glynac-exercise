COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE AGREEMENT (this "Lease") is made as of this 1st day of January, 2026, by and between GLOBAL PROPERTIES LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Landlord"), and INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS INC., a California corporation ("Tenant").

1. PREMISES.
Landlord hereby leases to Tenant, and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord, the premises located at 123 Business Park Blvd, Suite 400, Tech City, CA 94000 (the "Premises"), consisting of approximately 15,000 rentable square feet.

2. TERM.
The term of this Lease shall be for ten (10) years, commencing on January 1, 2026 (the "Commencement Date") and ending on December 31, 2035 (the "Expiration Date"), unless sooner terminated as provided herein.

3. RENT.
Tenant shall pay to Landlord as Base Rent the sum of $50,000.00 per month, subject to an annual increase of 3% appearing on each anniversary of the Commencement Date. Rent is due on the first day of each calendar month.

4. USE.
The Premises shall be used for general office purposes, software development, and related administrative uses, and for no other purpose without Landlord's prior written consent.

5. ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING.
Tenant shall not assign this Lease or sublet any portion of the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent, which consent may be withheld in Landlord's sole and absolute discretion. Any attempted assignment or sublease without consent shall be void and a default under this Lease.

6. REPAIRS AND MAINTENANCE.
Tenant shall, at its sole cost and expense, keep and maintain the Premises in good condition and repair. Landlord shall be responsible for the structural portions of the building and building systems.

7. INDEMNIFICATION.
Tenant shall indemnify, defend, and hold Landlord harmless from any and all claims, damages, liabilities, and expenses arising out of Tenant's use of the Premises or any breach of this Lease by Tenant.

8. INSURANCE.
Tenant shall maintain during the Term commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000 per occurrence.

9. DEFAULT.
The occurrence of any of the following shall constitute a default by Tenant: (a) failure to pay Rent when due; (b) abandonment of the Premises; (c) failure to perform any other provision of this Lease if not cured within 30 days after notice methods.

10. GOVERNING LAW.
This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
